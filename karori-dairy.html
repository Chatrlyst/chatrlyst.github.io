How Karori Dairy used Chatrlyst to boost its TikTok following by 30% and launch a YouTube channel, in just 2 months

"Before Chatrlyst, we had about 30,000 followers, and in just two months, we've soared past 40,000. We're not only producing better content but also saving a significant amount of time in the process"
Lukas Halikias and Rithwick Negi, the dynamic duo behind the popular Karori Dairy TikTok channel, achieved an impressive 30% increase in followers and successfully launched a YouTube series within merely two months of utilising Chatrlyst. Since its inception on TikTok in August 2022, 'The First Dairy' has amassed nearly 20 million views, 40,000 followers, and 1.2 million likes by March 2024. Check out their TikTok here.
The Journey to a 30% increase in followers in two months
Chatrlyst enabled Karori Dairy to swiftly pinpoint opportunities directly from their audience's feedback. The AI's insightful analysis showed them that their audience was highly engaged with sports, in particular football. Inspiring them to collaborate with well-known Wellington sports teams like the Hurricanes and Phoenix, leveraging their connections to enhance content relevancy and engagement.

This audience-inspired content strategy led to 1.5M views, 120k likes, and 2k comments along with a 30% increase in followers in just 2 months.
"The first time I tried Chatrlyst, I was astounded. It felt like it was reading my mind. I immediately rang Paul (Chatrlyst Co-Founder) and excitedly shared my amazement over the phone" - Lukas Halikias.
But how do they measure success?
"The ability to launch our new content series and quickly evaluate what resonates allows us to seize any emerging opportunities promptly," - Lukas Halikias
This agile approach to content creation and adjustment is key to their continued success and growth, building an engaged audience that goes beyond just TikTok trends.
What Lies Ahead for Karori Dairy?
Lukas and Rithwick are not resting on their laurels; they have embarked on a new venture, establishing a creative agency, MarkHit. Their mission is to bring viral content creation to major brands and television, fuelled by the insights and efficiency gained from Chatrlyst.
